About Sadeth Lee | Realtor. Friend. Neighbor. Professional.

DRE #: 02011684  |  Phone #: (949) 274-0118

Sadeth Lee has been an Orange County resident for over 20 years. She graduated from Chapman University with a Bachelor Science Degree in Health Services in 1997. She left Cambodia in 1975 as a refugee. She and her family escaped the country with just the shirts on their back. Through hard work and determination, she became who she is today, a person who is not afraid to put her best foot forward and work hard for her clients in order for them to achieve their dream homes. She is a dedicated full-time real estate professional.

She serves Orange County, helping buyers and sellers find their perfect homes in Southern California. She is a member of the National Association of Realtors (NAR)'s Green Designation, this advanced training in green building and sustainable business practices help her seek out and understand market properties with green features.

She is also a member of Orange County Association of Realtors (OCAR), California Association of Realtors (CAR), and California Regional Multiple Listing Services (CRMLS); DRE # 02011684.

In her spare time, she enjoys cooking, gardening, and traveling. She can speak Khmer fluently. She is also a member and volunteer of the Lake Forest Garden Club in Lake Forest, California.
